How this works

FinnBots is a Solana NFT collection. Pick-a-Bot runs on Solana — you'll need a Solana wallet (Phantom recommended) to play for real $OIL.

The odds. Ten Bots in the line-up, one drawn each round — 1 in 10, every round, the same for everyone whether you're playing free or for real $OIL.

Free Play. No wallet, no cost. Pick a Bot and play, purely for fun.

$OIL Play. Anyone holding at least 25 $OIL can play — connect your wallet and pay 25 $OIL to enter. Win, and 200 $OIL is yours, paid in the next weekly batch (same as emissions, not instantly). Hold a FinnBot and a win pays 1.5× — 300 $OIL instead of 200. Your Bot holding is re-checked when the batch is paid.

Payment. You sign exactly one transaction: your 25 $OIL entry fee. Nothing else is ever requested, and nothing is taken unless you choose to play.

Wallet safety. Connecting only lets us read your $OIL balance and which Bots you hold — that's it. We'll never ask for your seed phrase or private key, and your wallet only ever prompts you once, for the entry payment itself, which you approve or decline each time.
